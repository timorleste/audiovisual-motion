CAMSTL

Centro Audiovisual Max Stahl Timor Leste

Our mission is to help facilitate the newly independent East-Timor in accessing the voices and the key events in its past and fostering an understanding of its unique cultural heritage; to help unite and secure the identity of the country and its people; and to contribute to the forging of a new democratic nation. To ensure the survival of unique resources which contain and communicate the experience and the story of Timor-Leste and to place these at the service of peace and understanding, in Timor-Leste, Regionally and Internationally.
